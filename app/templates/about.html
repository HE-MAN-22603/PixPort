About PixPort

Making professional passport photos accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Mission

At PixPort, our mission is to simplify the process of creating compliant passport photos. We believe that getting a passport photo should be a quick, easy, and affordable process, not a chore. By leveraging the latest in artificial intelligence and computer vision, we provide a tool that is both powerful and user-friendly.

The Technology Behind PixPort

PixPort is built on a modern, robust technology stack to ensure a seamless and secure experience. Our backend is powered by Python and Flask, providing a solid foundation for our AI services. We use state-of-the-art machine learning models for background removal and photo enhancement, ensuring high-quality results.
