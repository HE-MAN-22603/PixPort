Frequently Asked Questions

What photo formats do you support?
We support JPG, JPEG, PNG, HEIC, and WebP formats. Your images are processed using advanced AI technology for the best results.
Is my photo data secure?
Yes! Your privacy is our top priority. All uploaded photos are automatically deleted after processing, and we never store your personal images permanently.
Which passport sizes do you support?
We support passport photo requirements for over 60 countries including US, UK, Canada, Australia, India, and many more.
How accurate is the AI background removal?
Our AI uses state-of-the-art machine learning models that achieve over 95% accuracy in background removal, even with complex hair details and edges.
